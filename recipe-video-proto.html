The Best Easy Pasta Salad

Meet your new summer bestie: A stupidly easy pasta salad with pasta, juicy tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, red onion, salami, olives, herbs, and a drench of quick homemade dressing.

PLAY

Recipe

Prep Time20 mins
Cook Time15 mins
Total Time35 mins
Yield8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb rotini pasta, cooked al dente
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 6 oz spicy salami, diced
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoncini
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh herbs (basil + parsley)
  • 1 cup bottled or homemade Italian dressing
  • Salt & freshly‚Äëground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in well‚Äësalted water according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water until cool.
  2. Toss it together. In a large bowl, combine pasta, tomatoes, mozzarella, salami, pepperoncini, olives, onion, and herbs.
  3. Dress. Pour dressing over the salad, starting with about 3/4 cup. Toss to coat, taste, and add the remainder as needed.
  4. Season & serve. Finish with salt and plenty of black pepper. Chill for 30 minutes (if you can wait) for flavors to meld, then dig in!
